Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Instagram Bot Manager ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • API Credentials: Instagram/Facebook access tokens, OpenAI API keys
  • Configuration Data: Bot settings, response preferences, business context
  • Uploaded Files: PDFs containing business information for AI training

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Logs: Bot activity, response generation, error logs
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address (for security purposes)
  • Performance Metrics: Response times, success rates

2.3 Third-Party Data

  • Instagram Content: Comments and posts accessed through Meta Graph API
  • AI Responses: Content generated through OpenAI's API

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Connecting to Instagram and Facebook APIs
  • Generating AI responses to Instagram comments
  • Storing bot configuration and preferences
  • Providing debugging and monitoring capabilities

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve functionality
  • Monitoring for errors and performance issues
  • Developing new features and capabilities

3.3 Security and Compliance

  • Preventing unauthorized access
  • Detecting and preventing abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Third-Party Services

We share information with:

  • Meta/Facebook: Through their Graph API for Instagram access
  • OpenAI: For AI response generation
  • Cloud Providers: For hosting and infrastructure (data encrypted)

4.2 We Do Not Sell Data

  • We never sell your personal information
  • We do not share data for marketing purposes
  • We do not provide data to data brokers

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by:

  • Valid legal process or court order
  • Law enforcement requests (when legally required)
  • Protection of rights and safety

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access on need-to-know basis
  • API Key Protection: Credentials are securely encrypted
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing security assessments

5.2 Data Retention

  • Configuration Data: Stored while you use the Service
  • Logs: Kept for 30 days for debugging purposes
  • API Keys: Encrypted and stored only while needed

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Data: Access your configuration and logs through the interface
  • Modify Settings: Change bot behavior and preferences anytime
  • Export Data: Download your configuration and business context

6.2 Data Deletion

  • Stop Service: Discontinue use at any time
  • Delete Data: Request complete data deletion (see Data Deletion page)
  • Revoke Access: Remove API permissions through respective platforms

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 Local Storage

  • We use browser local storage for interface preferences
  • No tracking cookies are used
  • No third-party analytics or advertising trackers

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure:

  • Adequate protection through standard contractual clauses
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Secure transfer mechanisms

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy
